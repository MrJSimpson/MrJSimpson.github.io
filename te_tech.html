Mr. J's Tech Bench

Technology has been at the center of Joshua's life since he was 3 years old. His parents realized early what opportunities lay in the computer industry, and ensured Josh had access to computers at all times. During the 1990's, Joshua was allowed to network the family home. He obtained the skills from a local internet service provider from Silverdale, WA called T.S.C.Net (The Service Connection Network).

Since then he has followed his passion, studying whatever technology he could obtain. This page is dedicated to the current technology acquired and studied. This is currently a static page. However, this will ultimately function as a technology-based blog including everything from NASA to Zigbee.
